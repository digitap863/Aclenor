ABOUT US

ECTRADE EUROPE is a division of ECTRADE B.V., a Netherlands-based company providing reliable transport, freight, and supply chain solutions across Europe and international markets.

As a division of ECTRADE B.V., we combine strong logistics capabilities with overseas trade experience, supporting businesses with efficient cargo movement and cross-border trade operations. Our core focus is logistics and freight supported by global trade expertise.

OUR SERVICES

We provide comprehensive logistics and supply chain solutions tailored to your business needs, ensuring efficiency and reliability across all touchpoints.

ROAD FREIGHT

ECTRADE EUROPE delivers reliable road transport solutions for containerized and loose cargo. From pickup to final delivery, we manage every step with efficiency and care. Whether Full Truck Load (FTL) or Less-Than-Truckload (LTL), we ensure safe, timely, and cost-effective delivery through our trusted partner network.

AIR FREIGHT

ECTRADE EUROPE delivers efficient and reliable air freight solutions through our strong global partners network. We work closely with leading airlines to provide fast, flexible, and cost-effective shipping worldwide. Our experienced team offers a true one-stop-shop service, handling everything from time-critical shipments to complex cargo movements, with full customs support.

SEA FREIGHT

At ECTRADE EUROPE, we handle your ocean shipments from start to finish, ensuring your cargo moves safely, efficiently, and cost-effectively. Leveraging our global network of trusted agents and strong carrier partnerships, we secure competitive rates and advise on the best solution for your shipment—whether LCL (less than container load) or FCL (full container load). From small packages to large-scale cargo, we deliver tailored ocean freight solutions with speed, reliability, and care.

CUSTOM CLEARANCE

ECTRADE EUROPE’s dedicated team ensures your shipments clear customs smoothly and on time. With deep regulatory knowledge and strong local authority relationships, we handle both air and ocean customs clearance efficiently, so your cargo moves without delays. We navigate the complexities of international trade regulations to keep your business moving forward.

WAREHOUSING & DISTRIBUTION

ECTRADE EUROPE goes beyond freight forwarding—we store, manage, and distribute your goods with precision. From large pallets to small parts, our flexible warehousing, fulfilment, and value-added services keep your supply chain smooth and efficient. Our facilities are equipped to handle diverse needs, ensuring your products are stored safely and delivered accurately.

GLOBAL MARKET

ECTRADE EUROPE operates across key international markets, connecting businesses through efficient logistics and cross-border trade solutions. With a strong presence in Europe, the United Arab Emirates, India, and Turkey, we support the seamless movement of goods across major trade corridors.
Our regional expertise allows us to manage complex supply chains, coordinate international freight, and support import and export activities with reliability and precision. By leveraging local market knowledge and strong partner networks, ECTRADE EUROPE ensures smooth operations, regulatory compliance, and timely delivery across borders.
