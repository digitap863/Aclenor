ABOUT US

ECTRADE EUROPE (A Division of ECTRADE BV)

ECTRADE EUROPE is a division of ECTRADE BV, a Netherlands-based company providing reliable transport, freight, and supply chain solutions across Europe and international markets.

As a division of ECTRADE BV, we combine strong logistics capabilities with overseas trade experience, supporting businesses with efficient cargo movement and cross-border trade operations. Our core focus is logistics and freight supported by global trade expertise.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver reliable, efficient, and integrated logistics and cross-border trade solutions that enable our customers to operate seamlessly across global markets. We strive to add value through operational excellence, strong partnerships, and a customer-centric approach.

SERVICES

ECTRADE EUROPE specializes in freight forwarding and logistics solutions designed to support European and international supply chains. We coordinate shipments efficiently, ensuring reliability, compliance, and cost control throughout the transport process.

AIR-FREIGHT

ECTRADE EUROPE delivers efficient and reliable air freight solutions through our strong global partners network. We handle everything from time-critical shipments to complex cargo movements with full customs support.

SEA FREIGHT

We manage your ocean shipments end-to-end ensuring your cargo moves efficiently and safely. LCL or FCL, we guide you to the right solution with competitive rates and carrier partnerships.

ROAD TRANSPORT

Reliable transport solutions for containerized and loose cargo (FTL & LTL). We manage every step, from order pickup to final delivery, ensuring safety and schedule compliance.

CUSTOMS CLEARING

Dedicated professionals ensure your consignments clear customs hassle-free. We take full ownership of air and ocean clearance, leveraging deep local regulatory knowledge.

WAREHOUSING & DISTRIBUTION

Flexible warehousing solutions for large pallets or small parts. Our services include handling, fulfilment, and value-added solutions to optimize your supply chain.

GLOBAL MARKET

ECTRADE EUROPE operates across key international markets, connecting businesses through efficient logistics and cross-border trade solutions. With a strong presence in Europe, the United Arab Emirates, India, and Turkey, we support the seamless movement of goods across major trade corridors.

Our regional expertise allows us to manage complex supply chains, coordinate international freight, and support import and export activities with reliability and precision.

CROSS BORDER TRADE

ECTRADE B.V. is a global merchant trading, procurement, and supply company with world-class credit and financing capabilities. We trade a broad spectrum of commodities across agricultural products, ferrous and non-ferrous metals, chemicals, steaming coal, and building materials.

We are a specialized, multi-disciplined procurement and supply company, delivering complete single-source procurement solutions globally. We supply machinery, equipment, and materials for construction, infrastructure, production, manufacturing, and various other sectors.
