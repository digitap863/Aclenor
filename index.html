Welcome To Aclenor Trading Pte. Ltd.

Aclenor Trading Pte. Ltd. is a global merchant trading, procurement and supply company with world class credit and financing capabilities. We trade a broad spectrum of commodities Like Agricultural Commodities (Plam oil, Soyabean, Pulses, Oil Seeds & Edible Oils, White Refined Sugar and Raw Sugar), Ferrous and Non - Ferrous Metals, (Aluminium Cathodes, Copper Cathodes & Hot Roll Steel Coils/Sheet/Bars), Paint Chemicals, Steaming Coal, Fertilizers & Building Materials and have strong partnerships with leading suppliers across the world.

We have a team of professionals who assist our customers with their custom requirements, ensuring products and goods can be purchased on the credit terms that best match their business cycles. We aim at satisfaction of each of our customers.

We project an entrepreneurial approach based on developing long term relationships with our partners.
